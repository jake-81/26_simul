How to Use the Atomic Mass Simulator

  1. Click the "Select" button under either the left or right pan to choose which side to add atoms to.
  2. Click on the atom symbols (H, O, C, N) at the bottom to add atoms to the selected pan.
  3. Observe how the balance beam tilts based on the total mass on each side.
  4. Click on atoms in the pans to remove them.
  5. Use the "Reset" button to clear both pans and start over.

The goal is to understand how different atoms contribute to the overall mass and balance.
